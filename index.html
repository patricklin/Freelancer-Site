Custom Database

I, along with 7 other students worked with a local yoga studio to build them a custom functional database prototype. After determining what information and business needs the client had, we designed the database schema, built entity-relationship diagrams in Microsoft Visio, populated tables in Microsoft Access, and wrote the relevant SQL queries.

I primarily worked on the Visio diagrams, formatting database inputs and outputs to and from Excel, R, VBA, etc. with Python scripts, and generating a Google Maps heatmap to plot the studio's customer distribution.
